Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Kitchen renovations typically take 2-4 weeks, custom furniture 1-3 weeks, and complete interior design projects 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide design consultation?

Yes! We offer comprehensive design consultation services. Our team will work with you to understand your vision, provide expert recommendations, and create detailed designs before any work begins.

What types of wood do you work with?

We work with a wide variety of premium woods including teak, oak, mahogany, pine, and engineered woods. We'll help you choose the best material based on your project requirements, budget, and aesthetic preferences.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive warranties on all our work. Structural work comes with a 5-year warranty, while finishes and hardware have a 2-year warranty. We stand behind the quality of our craftsmanship.

Can you work within my budget?

We work with various budgets and will provide options to match your financial requirements. During consultation, we discuss budget expectations and provide solutions that deliver the best value for your investment.

Do you handle commercial projects?

Yes, we handle both residential and commercial projects including offices, restaurants, retail spaces, and hotels. Our team has experience with commercial requirements and timelines.
